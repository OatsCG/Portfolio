Dot Visualizer

JS, HTML5 Canvas

20,000 particles influenced by a 10x10 grid of vector cells. The design changes based on the section you're scrolled to. Try it!

Custom velocity and acceleration modifiers power the designs, as well as angular accelerations on each cell. For efficiency, the particles are drawn on a ctx ImageData object before being pushed to the canvas.
